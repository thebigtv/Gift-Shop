Who we are

A shop with a soul

"Every product in our shop has been chosen by hand — we only stock things we'd genuinely give to the people we love."

Bramble & Co opened its doors in 2016 with a simple idea: to create a beautiful, welcoming space where people could find truly thoughtful gifts made by independent British makers.

We visit makers at their studios, test every product ourselves, and only choose brands that share our values — quality, sustainability, and genuine craft.
